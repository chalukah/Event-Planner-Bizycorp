### Panel Purpose
This webinar is designed to help veterinary professionals redefine leadership, strengthen team communication, and build thriving practice cultures through expert insights on wellbeing and resilience.

### Brief Topic Description
Join us for an essential conversation about establishing sustainable boundaries in veterinary practice. Our expert panel will share practical strategies for recognizing burnout, setting clear work-life boundaries, and building a culture of balance that supports long-term career sustainability.

## Discussion Points

1. Recognizing early signs of compassion fatigue and burnout
2. Establishing clear work-life boundaries without guilt
3. Teaching clients to respect after-hours limits
4. The role of leadership in modeling balance for teams
5. Self-care practices that support long-term sustainability
